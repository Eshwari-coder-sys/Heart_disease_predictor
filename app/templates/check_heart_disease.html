Heart Disease Risk Predictor

Predict your likelihood of heart disease

Please fill out the following health indicators as accurately as possible. Once you submit, the system will predict your risk of having heart disease.

ℹ️ Feature Info (click to expand)
  • age: Age of the patient in years
  • sex: 1 = male; 0 = female
  • cp: Chest pain type
    • 0: Typical angina
    • 1: Atypical angina
    • 2: Non-anginal pain
    • 3: Asymptomatic
  • trestbps: Resting blood pressure (mm Hg)
  • chol: Serum cholesterol (mg/dl)
  • fbs: Fasting blood sugar > 120 mg/dl (1 = true; 0 = false)
  • restecg: Resting electrocardiographic results (0, 1, 2)
  • thalach: Maximum heart rate achieved
  • exang: Exercise-induced angina (1 = yes; 0 = no)
  • oldpeak: ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest
  • slope: Slope of the peak exercise ST segment
  • ca: Number of major vessels (0-3) colored by fluoroscopy
  • thal: Thalassemia (3 = normal; 6 = fixed defect; 7 = reversible defect)
